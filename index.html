About RNEUK

RNEUK is a celebration of Neukgu, the legendary wolf who escaped from Daejeon O-World and returned safely after 9 days of freedom. This incredible story captured the hearts of people worldwide, inspiring a community-driven memecoin that honors courage, resilience, and the spirit of adventure.

Built on Binance Smart Chain, RNEUK combines the viral nature of memes with blockchain technology, creating a token that represents freedom, community, and the unbreakable will to survive - just like Neukgu's remarkable journey.
